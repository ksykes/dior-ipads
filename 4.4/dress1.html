This cocktail dress is constructed of off-white silk organza and embroidered with clusters of light purple flowers and dark green leaves. The semi-full, A-line skirt ends about mid-calf. Dior’s signature cinched waist is achieved with a corset made from two layers of cotton tulle. A ruched bandeau across the breasts creates a soft, youthful neckline. Long white gloves complete the look.x
