Chart

Each chart recorded the designs in a collection by type, who made it, for whom, and with textile swatches. This ensured the ateliers’ work was evenly distributed and that the mannequins had time to change during daily presentations.

Montrealer, Ann Levitt (1927-2013), daughter of Hannah and Alvin Walker (President of Holt Renfrew), wore this dress on her honeymoon at Tower Isle, Jamaica (December 1950). She purchased it at Holt Renfrew for a November evening party in honour of Saidye Bronfman.
