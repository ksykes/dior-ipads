This evening gown is made of silvery Celanese acetate satin and features an intricate embroidered floral and foliate design made of metallic thread, pearls, beads, sequins, and Swarovski crystals. The neckline is decorated with a thin band of pearl and blue flowers and a pattern of narrow embroidered triangles finished with blue crystal teardrops. A structured bodice flows into a long full skirt with accented hips. Curvilinear forms trail down the dress, ending about mid-way down the skirt, and blue and violet flowers are featured throughout.x
