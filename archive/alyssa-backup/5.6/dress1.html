Young girl’s formal dress

Spring 1957

  • line: Christian Dior Original in Canada, Exclusive to Holt Renfrew & Co. Ltd., Spring 1957, made to order
  • occasion: Late day - evening
  • atelier: Holt Renfrew Montreal
  • textile: Silk organdy, cotton embroidery
  • FR
  • A
  • A

Designed for a young girl, this dress has five layers of petticoats that create the soft, semi-full skirt. The bodice features a higher-cut waistline, short sleeves, and a shallow V neckline appropriate for a coming of age young woman. The entire garment is covered with cotton embroidered, yellow-toned narcissi with golden centres.x
